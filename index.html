Our Story

A Taste of Arabia
in the Heart of Chennai

Founded with a deep passion for authentic Arabian hospitality, Fattoush Restaurant & Banquet has been serving the flavours of the Middle East to Chennai for over a decade. Our chefs bring time-honoured recipes from Saudi Arabia, Yemen, and the Levant directly to your table.

From the slow-cooked tenderness of our signature Mandi platters to the smoky perfection of our Al Faham grills, every dish is crafted with the finest imported spices and locally sourced fresh ingredients — because great food begins with great care.

We are more than a restaurant. We are a space for family, celebration, and community — complete with a fully equipped banquet hall for weddings, corporate events, and every milestone that deserves a feast.
